ABOUT

  • OUR MISSION

    The Chester County High School Theatre Department strives to empower students through the art of performing. Young performers are encouraged to reach into the depths of their imagination in order to bring stories to life. Through the art of dialogue, song, and dance, students learn skills necessary for the real world, such as improv and adaptation. The sky is the limit for any young actor wanting to pursue a career in musical theatre.
    "All the world's a stage..."
